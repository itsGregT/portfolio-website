About The Royal Oak Pub

The Royal Oak Pub is an old-world specialty beer pub with a lively, modern twist and charm. The Royal Oak Pub is sure to quench the thirst of our distinguished guests. The wide variety of beverage choices are complemented by an open seating area, delicious food and live entertainment. Check our entertainment schedule to follow your favorite bands and events. Ask about our seasonal brews!
