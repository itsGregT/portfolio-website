Delicious Brews — Fantastic Food

The Royal Oak Pub is a brewpub serving delicious food and craft beer from all across the country. We have a variety of spaces for friends and family to gather. Keep an eye on our events page for live music and events. We strive to support local artists so if you or anyone you know would like to play or host an event, just let us know!

Passion for beer, food, and local community is what sets us apart from the rest. Come join us for dinner and live entertainment. We are constantly find new local talent to come and play. Support local breweries!

Hosting an Event?

Host your wedding or party in our Pub. We have plenty of room for everyone and are more than happy to accommodate for the needs of everyone. We take reservations for large parties of 10 or more in our main dining room. Please call in advance so there will be plenty of room to accommodate your party.
